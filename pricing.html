Pricing

Understanding how our reference prices work and what is included in each trade term.

About Reference Prices

Prices shown in our catalog are past transaction price ranges based on actual deals completed during the reference period shown. They are provided as a guide only. Actual prices for your order will depend on current market conditions, auction prices at the time of purchase, and your specific requirements.

All reference prices are FOB (Free On Board) Japan. Freight and insurance are not included unless stated otherwise.

Trade Terms Explained

FOB
Free On Board
  • Vehicle cost
  • Port charges (Japan)
  • Customs clearance (Japan)
  • Shipping handling charge
Does not include ocean freight or container fee
C&F
Cost & Freight
  • Everything in FOB, plus:
  • Ocean freight to your port
Please provide your destination port for a C&F quote
CIF
Cost, Insurance & Freight
  • Everything in C&F, plus:
  • Marine insurance (total loss)
Insurance covers total loss until destination port
