How It Works

A simple, transparent process from inquiry to delivery of your Japanese used car.

What Are Reference Vehicles?

The vehicles shown in our catalog are past transaction examples. They are not currently in stock. Photos and prices are for reference purposes only, showing what we have previously sourced and exported. Use them to understand pricing and specifications, then submit a quote request for a similar vehicle.

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Browse the Catalog

Look through our reference vehicle catalog. Each listing shows past transaction prices, specifications, and reference periods to help you understand market pricing.

2

Submit a Quote Request

If you find a vehicle that matches your needs, click "Request Quote" or use our RFQ form. You can also contact us directly via WhatsApp for a faster response.

3

Pay Deposit (T/T)

To confirm your order, you will need to pay a deposit by Telegraphic Transfer (T/T). We accept payment in USD and Japanese Yen.

4

We Source Your Vehicle

After receiving your order and deposit, we purchase the vehicle from auction on your behalf. Our auction network access ensures competitive pricing and quality.

5

Photo Confirmation & Balance

After purchase, we send you photos of the actual vehicle for your confirmation. Once you are satisfied, we request the remaining balance payment.

6

Export & Shipping

After receiving full payment, we arrange export and book the vessel. We will provide shipment details immediately after the booking is confirmed.

7

Documents Sent

Once your car is on the vessel, we send shipping documents by email and also dispatch originals by EMS or DHL: Bill of Lading, Invoice, and Export Certificate.
