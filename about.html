Gloria Trading ‚Äî Exporter of Japanese Used Cars

Gloria Trading is a Japan-based company specialising in the export of Japanese used cars to customers around the world. Founded in 2006 and headquartered in Natori, Miyagi Prefecture, we have built a reputation for supplying high-quality right-hand drive vehicles at competitive prices.

Japan is widely regarded as one of the best sources of used cars globally. Japanese used cars are renowned for their low cost, excellent condition, and high specifications. We leverage our strong ties with domestic auction networks to source vehicles that precisely match our customers' requirements.

We specialise exclusively in Right-Hand Drive (RHD) vehicles, serving markets across Africa and other RHD regions. Our primary service destinations include Ghana (Tema), Nigeria (Lagos), and Benin (Cotonou).
